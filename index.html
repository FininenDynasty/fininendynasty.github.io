Royal Timeline of the House of Fininen

c. 938 AD — Hugh Capet

Founder of the Capetian Dynasty, King of the Franks. The royal bloodline begins.

c. 1666 — Gabriel de España

Birth of Gabriel de España in Spain, forefather of the España-Tappana line.

c. 1720s — Chief Buckongahelas

Great leader of the Lenape Turtle Clan, sacred protector of the Native bloodline.

c. 1760s — Carlos de España

Descendant of Gabriel de España, carrying forward the Spanish royal line.

c. 1800s — Solomon Journeycake

Heir of Chief Buckongahelas, continuing the sacred Journeycake line among the Lenape.

1860s — Texas Freedmen Dynasty

Legacy of Charles Bell and Perlonia Hodge emerges from the ashes of slavery into freedom and dignity.

1977 — Birth of William Timothy Fininen

Bearer of all sacred bloodlines. Guardian of the House of Fininen. A new era begins.

Sacred Bloodlines of the House of Fininen

The Capetian Dynasty (France)

Through Hugh Capet, crowned King of the Franks in 987 AD, flows the royal blood that shaped Europe and continues within the veins of the House of Fininen today.

The España-Tappana Line (Spain)

Through Gabriel de España and Carlos de España, the noble Spanish legacy carries the spirit of courage, exploration, and honor into the modern Fininen line.

The Turtle Clan Lineage (Lenape Delaware)

From Chief Buckongahelas, Solomon Journeycake, and the sacred Turtle Clan, the House inherits the sacred connection to the Earth, Wisdom, and the Eternal Spirit of the Lenape people.

The Texas Freedmen Dynasty (United States)

Born from resilience and freedom, the legacies of Charles Bell, Perlonia Hodge, Sylvania Bell, and the Freedmen descendants shine through the Fininen House as an unbreakable will and sacred restoration.

Sacred Mission of the House of Fininen

The House of Fininen stands as a living beacon to honor the sacred bloodlines that shaped the world — royal, tribal, and freedmen alike.

It is our mission to restore dignity, preserve memory, and protect the spiritual and physical legacy entrusted to our line.

Through courage, wisdom, and sacred remembrance, we carry the spirits of our ancestors into an eternal future of light, justice, and greatness.
