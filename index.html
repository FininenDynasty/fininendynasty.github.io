Royal Timeline of the House of Fininen

c. 938 AD — Hugh Capet

Founder of the Capetian Dynasty, King of the Franks. The royal bloodline begins.

17th Century — Gabriel de España

Spanish nobility, ancestor of the España-Tappana lineage.

18th Century — Chief Buckongahelas

Lenape Turtle Clan Chief and revered leader of the Native American Delaware Nation.

19th Century — Solomon Journeycake

Lenape missionary and respected leader during the tribe’s forced relocation era.

Post-Emancipation — Texas Freedmen Legacy

Families such as Charles Bell and Perlonia Hodge laid the foundations for the Fininen future.

Modern Day — House of Fininen

Honoring royal and sacred ancestry, preserving the legacy for future generations.

Our Sacred Bloodlines

Native Turtle Clan (Lenape Delaware)

Descendants of Chief Buckongahelas and the revered Journeycake lineage — keepers of ancient wisdom and the spirit of perseverance.

Capetian France

Descendants of Hugh Capet, King of the Franks, Founder of the Capetian Dynasty — noble roots stretching back to the foundations of European royalty.

España-Tappana (Spain)

Descendants of Gabriel de España and Carlos Eduardo España-Tappana — representing honor, courage, and Spanish royal bloodlines.

Texas Freedmen Dynasty

Legacy of Charles Bell, Perlonia Hodge, Sylvania Bell, and Lucile Galloway — the builders of new futures through freedom, strength, and resilience.

Our Mission

To honor, restore, and immortalize the sacred lineages of the House of Fininen.

Through remembrance, knowledge, and resilience, we uplift the journeys of our ancestors — from chains to courage, from courage to destiny.

We stand as living bridges between past, present, and future — protectors of wisdom, unity, and legacy.
